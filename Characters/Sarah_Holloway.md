# Character Profile: Sarah Holloway (Fragment_Stabilized)

## Identity
- **Full Name:** Sarah Holloway
- **Original Role:** Communications Officer, Hephaestus Rig
- **Current State:** Stabilized Memory Fragment (Hot-fixed with Elias's User Privileges)

## Nature
- **Visuals:** Ivory skin, burning violet eyes; her form is more "perfect" than a human's, lacking fine imperfections.
- **Abilities:** Inherited maintenance-level code manipulation. She can perform "System Audits" and stabilize local reality, but at the cost of Elias's stability.

## Narrative Role
- The "Living Weapon." She represents the potential for the crew to be "saved" but at the cost of the protagonist's humanity and physical existence.
